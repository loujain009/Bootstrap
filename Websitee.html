OUR STORY





We're just regular folks sharing the same passion for coffee and obsessed with consistency. But the journey is never about us. It's always been about coffee, people and all acts of happiness we experienced and shared around this neighbourhood and everything in between. On top of that, we're more than just a coffee shop. We're a fair trade coffee supporters aiming to take an active role in the ecosystem by helping to promote and grow the nation's specialty coffee industry. We love what we do and we're proud at it. To us, this is our way to contribute to the community .
